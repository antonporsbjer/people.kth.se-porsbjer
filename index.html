About Me

My name is Anton Porsbjer and I am a computer science and engineering student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology from Stockholm, Sweden. I have a great passion for developing video games, fun software and web applications. I am currently pursuing my master's degree in computer science with a specialization in computer graphics and interaction.
